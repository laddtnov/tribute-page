Antoni Gaudí

The genius who turned architecture into art

Timeline of Antoni Gaudí's Life

  • 1852 Born in Reus, Catalonia, Spain
  • 1878 Graduated from Barcelona School of Architecture
  • 1883 Began work on Sagrada Família
  • 1900 Started construction of Park Güell
  • 1904 Renovation of Casa Batlló
  • 1906 Began construction of Casa Milà (La Pedrera)
  • 1926 Tragically died after being struck by a tram in Barcelona
  • 1984 His works recognized as UNESCO World Heritage Sites
